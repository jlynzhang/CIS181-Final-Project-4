Recipe Title:

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Heat a grill or grill pan over medium heat. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes on each side until cooked through and juices run clear.

Step 3: While the chicken is grilling, prepare the salad by tossing the greens, cucumber, and red onion in a bowl.

Step 4: Slice the grilled chicken and place on top of the salad.

Step 5: Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, then toss to combine. Serve immediately!

Recipe Title:

Vegetarian Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup spinach leaves
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced

Instructions:

Step 1: Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface.

Step 2: Spread a generous layer of hummus on the tortilla.

Step 3: Layer the shredded carrots, bell peppers, spinach, and avocado evenly over the hummus.

Step 4: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly.

Step 5: Slice the wrap in half and serve!

Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step 2: Add the chopped tomatoes and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.

Step 3: Use an immersion blender or transfer the soup to a blender to puree until smooth.

Step 4: Stir in the fresh basil and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot!
