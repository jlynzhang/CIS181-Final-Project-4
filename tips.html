Tips & Tricks

Welcome to our collection of essential cooking tips! Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned cook, these handy tricks will help save time, improve your skills, and make your meals even better.

General Cooking Tips

  • Read the recipe first: Ensure you have all ingredients ready before starting to cook.
  • Invest in a good knife: A sharp knife makes chopping easier and safer.
  • Season as you go: Taste your food while cooking and adjust the seasoning gradually.

Easy Ingredient Substitutions

Ran out of an ingredient? Here are some quick and easy swaps you can use:

  • No Butter? Use an equal amount of coconut oil or olive oil.
  • No Buttermilk? Mix 1 cup of milk with 1 tablespoon of vinegar or lemon juice.
  • No Eggs? Use 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce or mashed banana as a replacement in baking.

Food Storage & Leftover Hacks

Quick Tips:

  • Store herbs like cilantro in a glass of water, covered loosely with a plastic bag, in the fridge.
  • Keep avocados fresh by storing with the pit and brushing the surface with lemon juice.
  • Freeze leftover broth or sauces in ice cube trays for easy portioning.
  • Place bread slices in the freezer to prevent staleness; toast straight from frozen when needed.
