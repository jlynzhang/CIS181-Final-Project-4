Recipe of the Week

Turkey and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (whole wheat, white, or your choice)
  • 2-3 slices of turkey breast (deli-sliced)
  • 1-2 slices of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your preference)
  • Lettuce (optional)
  • Tomato slices (optional)
  • Mustard or mayonnaise (optional)
  • Butter (optional, for toasting)

Instructions:

Step 1: Place one slice of bread on a flat surface.

Step 2: Layer the slices of turkey breast on top of the bread.

Step 3: Add a slice or two of your chosen cheese on top of the turkey.

Step 4: If desired, add lettuce and tomato slices for extra freshness.

Step 5: Optionally, spread mustard or mayonnaise on the second slice of bread.

Step 6: Place the second slice of bread on top to close the sandwich.

Step 7: (Optional) Spread butter on the outer sides of the bread and toast the sandwich on medium heat for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.

Step 8: Cut the sandwich in half!

Step 9: Enjoy your meal!
